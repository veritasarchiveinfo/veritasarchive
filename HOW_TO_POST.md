# How to Post

Thinking of posting a new intelligence brief? It's simple.

1.  **Navigate** to `src/content/blog/`.
2.  **Create** a new file, e.g., `my-new-post.md`.
3.  **Paste** the following template at the top:

```markdown
---
title: 'Your Title Here'
description: 'A brief summary of the post.'
pubDate: 'Feb 15 2026'
heroImage: '/placeholder-1.jpg'
category: 'Analysis'
---

Start writing your post content here...
```

4.  **Write** your content in standard text (Markdown).
5.  **Save**. The website automatically updates!

## Tips
- **Images**: Put images in `public/` folder and reference them like `/image.jpg`.
- **Formatting**:
    - `**Bold**` for bold
    - `*Italic*` for italic
    - `> Quote` for quotes
